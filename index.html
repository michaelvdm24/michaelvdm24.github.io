About Me

Hello, I'm Michael! I am an electrical engineer with a focus in automatic control and a background in power & energy systems. I enjoy problem solving, rock n' roll, and rowing. For me, engineering is grounded in the desire to better the world we live in with problem solving, whether that be running power factor tests on transformers or designing microcontrollers for robots. The website you are looking at is the product of my obsession with Python and r/dataisbeautiful.

I attended Purdue University for my undergraduate years. I was fortunate enough to take away many great memories. My time there was occupied with late-nights — beating nonlinear system equations to death with differentials equations, freezing in sub-zero weather on a seven shell with teammates, and trying to find undervalued options using Black-Scholes. Albeit a tumultuous time of staring at problem sets and asking the same reworded question 6 times at TA office hours, I would say what truly stuck with me are the challenges completed outside the lecture room.

These challenges have formed the person I am today. A hardworking and inquisitive engineer eager to accept a challenge. My thoughts center around three pillars: (1) communication, relationship building, and emotional intelligence; (2) bringing value to the world with technology, and (3) learning something new every day. Thank you for taking the time to go through my site!
